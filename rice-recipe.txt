Ingredients:
- 3 onz chopped onion
- one cup rice
- one cups water
- one cup chicken broth
- half teaspoon salt
- one spoon live oil

Directions:
Stir the onion into the rice; cook and stir 1 minute more. Pour chicken broth and water over the rice mixture, add salt, and bring to a boil; reduce heat to low, place a cover on the saucepan, and cook until the broth is absorbed and the rice is tender, about 20 minutes.
